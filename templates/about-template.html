Our Company

Gopika Perfumery Works is a Mysore based Manufacturer and Exporter of Incense Sticks and Dhoops. Mysore is home to two of the most natural and popular of fragrances, namely Sandal and Jasmine. And the company was established in the year 1956 at this fragrance backdrop. Late S.R. Lakshminarasimhaiah was the brainchild behind the inception of the company as well as the company's status as a household name. Currently the company is steered by his son Sri. S.L. Ramachandra who has been successfully guiding the company to new horizons. The company is also committed to the cause of the society by helping rural women with employment opportunities for a better livelihood.

Our Brand

GOPIKAA is a brand synonymous with quality and aromatic Incense Sticks and Dhoops. These products are genuine and non-toxic, and are made from herbal extracts and oils. Marketed under the brand name of 'GOPIKAA ', our Incense Sticks and Dhoops are greatly popular both in the domestic and international market. GOPIKAA is known for its unique fragrant blends. You can choose amongst varied fragrances right from Jasmine, Rose, Sandal, Lilly, Lavender, Vanilla, Green Breeze to other fragrances in our range to suit different occasions.
